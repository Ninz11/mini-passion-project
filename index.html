Coffee is a beverage brewed from roasted, ground coffee beans. Darkly colored, bitter, and slightly acidic, coffee has a stimulating effect on humans, primarily due to its caffeine content. Coffee has the highest sales in the world market for hot drinks. The "beans" are roasted and then ground into fine particles, which are typically steeped in hot water before being filtered out. It is usually served hot, although chilled or iced coffee is common.
